Senior Data Engineer
at Meridian Loop

Meridian Loop is building a modern data platform that powers customer intelligence, product analytics, and operational reporting for a fast-growing B2B software business. We are looking for a Senior Data Engineer who can design reliable pipelines, model clean datasets, and partner closely with analytics, product, and business teams.

Responsibilities
- Build and maintain scalable batch and streaming data pipelines that ingest data from application databases, third-party APIs, and event streams.
- Design, implement, and optimize ELT workflows using Python, SQL, and orchestration frameworks such as Airflow.
- Develop dimensional models and curated warehouse tables that support product analytics, finance reporting, and executive dashboards.
- Improve data quality, observability, and operational excellence through testing, alerting, documentation, and root-cause analysis.
- Collaborate with analysts, software engineers, and business stakeholders to translate ambiguous requirements into trusted datasets and self-service data products.
- Contribute to platform architecture decisions involving AWS, Snowflake, data lake storage, and CI/CD practices.

Requirements
- 5+ years of experience building production-grade data pipelines and warehouse models.
- Strong proficiency in Python and SQL for transformation, automation, and data processing.
- Hands-on experience with Airflow or similar orchestration tools in production environments.
- Experience working with cloud data platforms such as AWS, Snowflake, Redshift, or Databricks.
- Familiarity with distributed processing technologies such as Spark or Flink.
- Demonstrated ability to improve data reliability, monitoring, and pipeline performance.
- Ability to communicate clearly with technical and non-technical stakeholders.

Preferred Qualifications
- Experience supporting product analytics or SaaS business intelligence use cases.
- Familiarity with dbt, Terraform, or infrastructure-as-code workflows.
- Experience designing data quality frameworks and automated validation checks.
- Comfortable mentoring junior engineers and driving best practices across the data stack.
